About Us

We are Alarcio, Relameda, Medellada, and Oro — classmates who became close friends over the course of a year. Through late-night study sessions, shared meals, and endless conversations, we discovered a common passion: good food and creating something meaningful.

From that shared dream, Almedoro was born — a restaurant that brings together flavors we love, crafted with Filipino warmth and heart. At Almedoro, every meal tells a story of friendship, passion, and the joy of sharing good food with others.
